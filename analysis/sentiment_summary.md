# Sentiment Analysis Summary

## Overview
This document summarizes the sentiment distribution across 100 X (Twitter) posts related to Maldives digital transformation collected between September 2024 and December 2025.

---

| Sentiment | Count | Percentage |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| Positive | 75 | 75% |
| Neutral | 15 | 15% |
| Negative | 10 | 10% |
| **Total** | **100** | **100%** |

## Engagement by Sentiment

**Key Finding**: Positive posts receive highest average engagement, but negative posts generate more replies proportionally, indicating they spark discussion.

## Critical Negative Posts

The following posts represent significant critical discourse:

1. **Post_063** (148 likes, 86 reposts): Criticism of Google cable project as "political and economic loss"
2. **Post_026**: Questions realistic achievement of 15% GDP target by 2030
3. **Post_016**: Satirical post about "Maldives 0.2 corruption"
4. **Post_095**: Concerns about data privacy and protection laws

---

## Implications for Research

1. **Echo Chamber Effect**: Official accounts dominate positive narrative
2. **Citizen Voice**: Individual users provide critical counterbalance
3. **Limited Dialogue**: Low reply rates suggest one-way communication
4. **Digital Safety Concerns**: NGOs raise important warnings about online harms
